parippu

ingredients

  • Red lentils
  • Mustard seeds
  • Onion
  • Indian green chili
  • Garlic
  • Vegetable oil or butter
  • Salt
  • Turmeric
  • Chili powder
  • Coconut milk
  • Optionally, curry leaf
  1. Wash red lentils until the water runs clear and is free of debris.
  2. Add the lentils to a pot with a small amount of water and bring to a boil.
  3. While boiling, finely dice the red onion, garlic, and green chili.
  4. After boiling, remove the lentils and place them in a separate bowl.
  5. Over medium-high heat, add the oil or butter to the pot.
  6. Allow the oil to heat, then add the mustard seeds.
  7. When the mustard seeds begin popping, add the onion, garlic, and curry leaves.
  8. Season both the lentils and vegetables in the pot with chili powder, salt, and turmeric.
  9. Allow to sauté for 30–60 seconds, then reintroduce the lentils and stir.
  10. Dilute the coconut milk with water in a separate container, then add it to the pot; adjust as needed.
  11. Enjoy with rice, roti, etc.
