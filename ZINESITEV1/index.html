Sri Lankan Curry Powder

Recipe via Anoma's Kitchen on YouTube.

Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast/thigh
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Green chili
  • Sri Lankan curry powder
  • Turmeric
  • Black pepper
  • Chili powder
  • Coconut milk
  1. Cut chicken into small pieces and season with salt, pepper, and chili powder.
  2. In a separate bowl, add chopped tomato, red onion, green chili, garlic and curry leaves.
  3. Using a clay pot if you have one, add oil and put on stove on medium heat.
  4. When the pot is hot, add cut vegetables. Add curry powder, turmeric, black pepper, chili powder.
  5. Allow to sauté for a little bit, then add chicken with a bit of water. Cover and allow to boil.
  6. After allowing to boil until mostly cooked, add coconut milk, stir, and allow to boil until cooked through.

Parippu

Ingredients

  • Red lentils
  • Mustard seeds
  • Onion
  • Indian green chili
  • Garlic
  • Vegetable oil or butter
  • Salt
  • Turmeric
  • Chili powder
  • Coconut milk
  • Optionally, curry leaf
  1. Wash red lentils until the water runs clear and is free of debris.
  2. Add the lentils to a pot with a small amount of water and bring to a boil.
  3. While boiling, finely dice the red onion, garlic, and green chili.
  4. After boiling, remove the lentils and place them in a separate bowl.
  5. Over medium-high heat, add the oil or butter to the pot.
  6. Allow the oil to heat, then add the mustard seeds.
  7. When the mustard seeds begin popping, add the onion, garlic, and curry leaves.
  8. Season both the lentils and vegetables in the pot with chili powder, salt, and turmeric. Allow to sauté for 30–60 seconds, then reintroduce the lentils and stir.
  9. Dilute the coconut milk with water in a separate container, then add it to the pot; adjust as needed.
  10. Enjoy with rice, roti, etc.

Bandakka

Ingredients

  • Okra
  • Vegetable oil
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Chili powder
  • Turmeric
  • Red onion
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  1. Wash the okra.
  2. Cut off the top part of the okra, then chop the okra at an angle until the end.
  3. Mix with a pinch of turmeric and season with salt.
  4. Slice the onion and finely chop the garlic.
  5. Heat a skillet over low–medium heat and add the okra to toast and remove the slimy texture.
  6. Remove the okra from the skillet and place it in a separate bowl.
  7. Pour oil into the skillet, wait until heated, then add the garlic, onion, tomato, and salt.
  8. Once the onions are soft, add the okra back in for a few minutes.
  9. Add red pepper flakes, chili powder, and salt to taste.

Leeks

Ingredients

  • Leeks
  • Vegetable oil
  • Chili powder
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Turmeric
  • Red onion
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  1. Wash leeks thoroughly, removing any debris.
  2. Cut into thin slices.
  3. Pour oil in pan, over medium heat. Allow it to warm.
  4. Add onion & turmeric, cooking until translucent.
  5. Add leeks, mix well with onion and put heat on medium-low.
  6. Allow to cook for a few minutes and soften, then season with red pepper, salt, and chili powder.

Pol Sambol

Ingredients

  • Grated coconut
  • Red onion
  • Indian green chili
  • Lime
  • Chili powder
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  1. Add onions, green chilis, salt, black pepper, and chili powder to a mortar and pestle.
  2. Grind the mixture.
  3. Add the coconut to the mortar and pestle and crush together.
  4. Adjust seasoning to taste and finish with lime juice.
